**Input subtask:** "Generate 5 blog post ideas for an electrical services business targeting homeowners."

### Summary
Generated ideas targeting common homeowner questions around safety, cost, and home improvement. Each idea is search-intent driven and positions the business as a trusted local expert.

### Ideas
1. "5 Signs Your Home Wiring Needs an Urgent Safety Check" — safety-first, high search volume, drives emergency enquiries
2. "How Much Does a Consumer Unit Replacement Cost in 2026?" — pricing transparency, targets cost-conscious homeowners
3. "EV Charger Installation: What Homeowners Need to Know Before Booking" — growing demand, positions business as forward-looking
4. "Why You Should Never DIY Electrical Work (and What to Do Instead)" — authority piece, drives professional bookings
5. "Preparing Your Home's Electrics for a Loft Conversion" — renovation tie-in, targets high-value project enquiries

### Notes
- Ideas 1 and 4 can be combined into a safety hub page for SEO clustering
- Idea 3 is timely given UK EV adoption growth — prioritise for Q2

## Example 2: Outline Generation

**Input subtask:** "Create a page outline for a 'Consumer Unit Replacement' service page."

**Output:**

### Summary
Structured outline for a service landing page. Follows a trust-building flow: problem awareness → expertise signal → process transparency → social proof → CTA.

### Outline
1. **Hero section** — headline: "Consumer Unit Replacement — Done Safely, Done Right"
   - Sub-headline: certified electricians, fully insured, work guaranteed
   - CTA: "Get a free quote"

2. **Why replace your consumer unit?**
   - Old fuse boxes are a fire risk
   - Modern units add RCD protection
   - Required for home insurance in many cases

3. **Our replacement process** (3-step)
   - Step 1: Free survey and quote
   - Step 2: Scheduled installation (typically half a day)
   - Step 3: Full testing, certification, and sign-off

4. **What's included**
   - New consumer unit (brand options explained)
   - Full wiring check
   - Electrical Installation Certificate

5. **Customer reviews** — 2–3 testimonials specific to this service

6. **FAQs**
   - How long does it take?
   - Do I need to be home?
   - Will my power be off all day?

7. **CTA block** — "Ready to upgrade? Get your free quote today."

### Notes
- Add local area references in hero and FAQs for SEO (e.g. "serving [town] and surrounding areas")
- Certification badge (NICEIC/NAPIT) should appear near hero CTA for trust

### Confidence
High

## Example 3: Content Repurposing

**Input subtask:** "Repurpose this service page intro paragraph into: (a) a social media caption, (b) an email subject line, and (c) a meta description."

**Source material:** "At Herman Adu Electrical, we provide safe, reliable consumer unit replacements for homes across the local area. Our certified electricians carry out all work to the latest wiring regulations, leaving you with a fully tested installation and your electrical certificate."

**Output:**

### Summary
Repurposed source paragraph into three formats. Each adapts tone and length to channel conventions — social is conversational, email is benefit-led, meta is keyword-optimised.

### Repurposed Content

**(a) Social media caption (LinkedIn/Facebook):**
"Is your fuse box more than 20 years old? It could be putting your home at risk. We replace consumer units quickly and safely — certified, insured, and fully documented. Drop us a message for a free quote. #ElectricalSafety #HomeImprovement"

**(b) Email subject line options (pick one):**
- "Your fuse box might be a fire risk — here's what to do"
- "Consumer unit replacement: what's included and how long it takes"
- "Safe electrics, certified work — free quote this week"

**(c) Meta description (155 chars):**
"Need a consumer unit replacement? Herman Adu Electrical provides certified, fully tested installations. Safe, reliable work — get your free quote today."

### Notes
- Social caption works for both LinkedIn (professional tone) and Facebook (community tone)
- Email subject line option 1 is highest open-rate risk — test against option 3 if brand voice prefers lower pressure

### Confidence
High
