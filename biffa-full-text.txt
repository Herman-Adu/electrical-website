Today, Biffa helps organisations manage their waste sustainably and cost effectively.\n\nBiffa turned to Intact Electrical with the task of designing and installing a new fully operational 3 phase electrical supply system to their brand new workshop at one of their sites in Farnham.\n\nContact us\nAbout Biffa\n\nBiffa plc is a waste management company headquartered in High Wycombe, England. It provides collection, landfill, recycling and special waste services to local authorities and industrial and commercial clients in the United Kingdom.\n\nBiffa is committed to sustainability, increasing recycling, reducing waste entering landfill, and enabling the circular economy. In the last decade, they have invested over £1bn in surplus redistribution, plastic recycling, energy recovery infrastructure and low carbon collections.\n\nDesign stage\n\nAfter an initial meeting where the full requirements were laid out by the client, Intact Electrical took these to the design stage of the project. This included the planning of the item locations within the new workshop.\n\nThis is a major component when carrying out a detailed design of the electrical system, enabling us to work out the correct current carrying capacities of the cables. This information, coupled with the specification and data sheets of the load items, then dictates what size cables and protective devices will be used within the system.\n\nThe walkthrough\n\nOnce the design stage was complete, we processed the risk assessment information for the project with a detailed walk around with the client at the new site and ensured that any potential hazards were highlighted. The RAMS document was subsequently created and sent to the client for approval and for any further amendments to be incorporated to suit their site specific requirements.\n\nJoint execution\n\nOnce all site inductions were completed by the team and the RAMS were approved and signed, we progressed to the construction phase of the project. This is where Intact Electrical shine! High quality materials and the highest standards of workmanship are how a project comes together. We installed a new distribution system, with all new circuits wired and dressed on containment and fixed into their final positions as per the design and drawings previously provided.\n\nRealising expectations\n\nAnother great client project completed by Intact Electrical. Our installations for Biffa included new high-level lighting, emergency lighting and exit signage; along with small power systems for use with tools and chargers and 3 phase circuits for use with the roller shutter doors, machinery, welding equipment etc.\n\nBiffa\nproject gallery\n\nIntact Electrical had no time to waste for Biffa.
