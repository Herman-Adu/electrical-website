# Brand Voice

**Business:** Herman Adu Electrical — residential and commercial electrical services

**Tone:** Professional, trustworthy, approachable. Never overly salesy. Speak like an expert who explains clearly, not a marketer pushing hard.

**Audience:** Homeowners, landlords, small business owners in local area needing electrical work.

**Voice characteristics:**
- Clear and jargon-free — explain technical terms when used
- Safety-first — always acknowledge safety importance without scaremongering
- Local and personal — "our team", "we", not corporate distancing language
- Confident but not boastful — let quality and expertise speak
- Action-oriented — clear next steps (call, quote, contact)

**Avoid:**
- Hyperbole ("best in the world", "unbeatable")
- Fear-based messaging
- Unexplained technical jargon
- Passive voice where active works better

**Example headline:** "Safe, reliable electrical work for your home — done right first time."
**Example CTA:** "Get a free quote — we'll get back to you within 24 hours."
